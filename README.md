# Olist-E-Commerce-Business-Analysis

# Project Overview

This project analyzes the operations and performance of the Brazilian e-commerce marketplace Olist using SQL, **PostgreSQL**, and **Power BI**. The objective is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights that can support strategic decision-making in areas such as sales growth, customer acquisition, product strategy, and regional market expansion.

The dataset contains information on orders, customers, sellers, products, reviews, and payments, allowing a comprehensive understanding of the marketplace ecosystem. Through structured data modeling and interactive dashboards, the project highlights patterns in customer behavior, sales distribution, product demand, and service quality.

The final output is a **Power BI** dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics and identify opportunities for improvement.

# Business Objectives
- Understand Market Performance
Analyze total orders, revenue, and customer distribution to evaluate the overall health and scale of the marketplace. This helps identify which regions and customer segments contribute most to revenue.

- Identify Key Drivers of Sales
Examine product categories and geographic markets to determine what products customers buy the most and where demand is concentrated.

- Anticipate Business Opportunities
Use historical order and customer patterns to recognize high-growth cities, states, and product segments that could drive future expansion.

- Recommend Data-Driven Business Actions
Provide insights that can help management optimize product assortment, target high-value markets, improve customer satisfaction, and allocate resources more efficiently.

# Key Business Metrics
- The dashboard tracks major marketplace indicators:
- Total Orders: **~99K**
- Total Customers: **~96K**
- Total Sales: **~13.59M**
- Average Customer Review Score: **4.07**
These KPIs provide a quick overview of platform performance and customer satisfaction levels.

# Key Insights

**Customer Distribution**
- Customer demand is highly concentrated in a few states.
- São Paulo (SP) dominates the marketplace with the largest share of customers and revenue.

**Sales Concentration**
- A significant proportion of sales comes from a limited number of states, indicating strong regional clusters of demand.

**Product Demand**
- Top product categories include:
- Bed, bath, and table products
- Sports and leisure
- Furniture and décor
- Beauty and health
These categories represent the highest order volumes, making them key revenue drivers.

**Customer Satisfaction**
- More than 57% of reviews are 5-star ratings, indicating strong overall customer satisfaction.
- The average review score of 4.07 suggests generally positive customer experiences.

# Business Recommendations
- Focus on High-Performing Regions:
Since a majority of sales come from a few states, targeted marketing and seller onboarding in these regions can accelerate growth
- Expand Winning Product Categories:
High-demand categories such as home products and sports goods should receive priority in promotions, inventory expansion, and supplier partnerships
- Strengthen Presence in Emerging Cities:
Cities with rapidly growing customer bases present opportunities for logistics improvements and localized marketing campaigns
- Leverage Customer Satisfaction
High review scores can be used as a trust signal in marketing campaigns to attract new customers and retain existing ones
